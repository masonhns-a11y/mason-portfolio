About

A dynamic and versatile animator with over three years of professional experience delivering character and creature animation for film, television, advertising, and games.

Previously a Visualisation Artist at The Third Floor on two upcoming Disney feature productions — handling scene layout, camera animation, and character blocking within a major studio pipeline.

Highly proficient in Autodesk Maya and its ecosystem, with real-time experience in Unreal Engine 5 for previs. Strong track record on high-profile titles including a BAFTA-nominated short film selected for the Annecy Film Festival.

Currently mentoring the next generation of animators at Ravensbourne University through live sessions, recorded content, and hands-on feedback.
